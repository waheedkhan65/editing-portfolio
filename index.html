Professional Video Editor

I am Waheed, a seasoned video editor with over 5 years of professional experience, skilled in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and a suite of other industry-standard tools — transforming raw ideas into visually stunning, emotionally engaging content that connects.

I specialize in crafting scroll-stopping shorts, viral cash cow videos, cinematic vlogs, immersive ASMR edits, podcasts, and educational content — all tailored to captivate audiences and boost your brand presence.
